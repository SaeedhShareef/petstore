Welcome to Sandra's Pet Paradise! Located in beautiful sunny Cincinnati Ohio, our lovable furry friends are ready and waiting to find a home with You!

Here at Sandra's, we focus on rehabilitating animals and keeping them in a safe, loving and protected environment.

We treat the animals in our care just like family. We keep them loved and protected for a smooth transition from our home to yours.

We've been raising animals all over the tri-state area for nearly 60 years! With 3 generations of love behind our mission, we strive to find a perfect fit for every family.
