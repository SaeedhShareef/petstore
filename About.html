Meet Sandra

Sandra, also affectionately known as "Nana", has been taking care of pets ever since she was a little girl. One faitful day,

she found a lost kitten on the street near her elementary school and just knew she had to take her home and fix her up

as best she could. Sadly, after a few weeks she wasn't able to keep the kitten but she knew just the neighbor that

could! Thankfully she was still able to visit and play with her feline friend, "Molasses" every day and she quickly learned

that the bond between her family, and her neighbors, had only grown stronger! The spark inside of her heart became

an inextinguishable fire, and she dedicated the rest of her life to helping animals. At the tender age of 70 years young,

she's got no signs of stopping!

Let's meet the rest of the family!

Meet Carolyn

To say that Sandra's oldest Daughter, Carolyn, is a wiz in the kitchen, would be putting it lightly. After graduating from

the Midwest Culinary Institute, and doing some much needed traveling around the globe, she decided to come back

home and embark (get it?) on a new challenge! She created a line of gourmet, organic dog treats, for the family pet

store! You'll see all kinds of cupcakes, doughnuts and delectable treats for your furry loved ones, in our Shop section!

Meet Jacqueline

As the old saying goes, the apple doesn't fall far from the tree. Jacqueline has worked for nearly 20 years as a groomer!

Whether it's cats or dogs, you name it, she'll groom it! A big part of rehabilitation for our animals is making sure they're

healthy and clean! Matted fur can cause animals bloodclots or even provide a nest for ticks and fleas. Jacqueline's

mission is to make sure that the animal that you choose is just as cute as the day they were born.

Meet Ava

Sandra's granddaughter Ava may be the baby of the family, but she's got a heart that's full grown. At 13 years old,

she's already making waves as an animal rights activist, with both online campaigns, and if need be, in person protests!

She's never afraid to fight for what she believes in, and she'll certainly tell you herself! Nana couldn't be more proud!
